Cervical Spine Protocol
▾
🔥 Every Session — Warm-Up
Cervical chin tucks — 2×10 reps, 3s hold. Gently retract chin, no force.
Lateral neck tilt stretch — ear to shoulder. Never force ROM.
Upper trap & levator scap stretch — pull head diagonally.
🧘 Every Session — Cooldown
Cervical rotation stretch — slow chin to each shoulder.
Child's pose / thoracic decompression — arms extended 45s.
Doorway pec stretch + chin tuck simultaneously — postural reset.
⛔ Exercises to Avoid
Behind-neck press or pull
Upright row (compresses cervical discs)
Heavy shrugs with jerky movement
Neck bridging of any kind
💪 Strengthening (3× per week — Fri warm-up)
Isometric neck — hand resists forehead & temples. 10s holds.
Deep neck flexor activation — nod chin while lying flat.
Face pulls — elbows HIGH. Rear delts + deep cervical stabilisers.

Progressive Overload Rules
▾
✅ All sets completed last week
Add +2.5kg next session. Tracker auto-suggests this.

Hold the same weight. Don't add load until all sets are done.

Deload: drop 10% weight. Rebuild over 2 weeks, then progress again.
